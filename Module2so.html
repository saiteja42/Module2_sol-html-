Our Menu

Chicken

A chicken nugget is a chicken product made from chicken meat that is breaded or battered, then deep-fried or baked. Invented in the 1950s, they have become a popular fast food restaurant item, as well as commonly sold as a frozen product for home use.

Sushi

This kind of sushi comes in a cone-shape, created by the nori wrapped around the ingredients inside. They are usually filled with a mixture of rice, fish, and vegetables. Sushi can be eaten with hands or chopsticks. Soy sauce and wasabi are commonly eaten with sushi.

Sake

The rest of rice is steamed and added to a fermentation starter and a fermentation mash. Sake is an alcoholic beverage made from rice. There are special varieties of sake rice that are suitable for sake making, called shuzo-kotekimai, just as wine is made from wine grapes.
